Hey there, I’m Christa Rodriguez, a freelance UX researcher who’s learned enough to be dangerous with UI design. I specialize in empathizing with users and clearly communicating their needs in a language design teams can understand.

My skills include teamwork, project management, Figma, the Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe products, and always being early.

UX Skills

User Research

I like to have broad quantitative data to dial in my interviews. Anthropology makes ethnography second nature to me, but UX training has let me streamline the process.

User Empathy

A person can tell you what they think they want, but you have to listen to the spaces between their words to understand how their feelings inform what they’re saying.

Qualitative Interviews

I love to get people to tell me their stories. That’s where the real insight comes from. When you lead them to a place they feel comfortable, they’ll tell you exactly what they think.

Case Studies

Madison Roller Derby Site Redesign

This website redesign focused on bringing balance the athleticism of roller derby with the personality it is known for. Direct league interaction and feedback were utilized to create something to draw in fans and direct them to ticket sales and merchandise.

Sage Garden Advice App

This project focued on reserching gardeners’ pain points and how they could be address with the creation of an app that condenced multiple resouces into one. The guidence provided benefits gardeners of all levels, and the calming aesthetics of the app helps create a more relaxing gardening experience.

Untethered Workcation App

A research focused project, gathering information on what people would like to see in a travel app for a post pandemic world. The reserch was then turned into a Mid-Fi prototype with a focus on creating a user friendly onbarding process.
